Which Latin South American Country Should YOU Go To: an Interactive Quiz

Background photo taken by Micaela Leiva on January 6th, 2020.

LET'S GET STARTED...

1. Which of the following sound more appealing to you?

2. Which food would you rather eat right now? (choose only one)

3. Which European city do you like the most?

4. Do you like crowded cities?

5. Do you speak spanish?

6. Do you usually drink coffee?

7. Assuming you are from the U.S., can you tolerate long flights??

8. What's your ideal weather like?

9. Last but not least, why would you want to visit South America?

Some code written by Keyley Seow on Github.

Brazil is the largest country in South America and the fifth largest country in the world, so you are most likely a people person!. You probably enjoy partying during the weekends and having fun with your friends. Maybe spanish isn't your first language but you can still communicate perfectly since most locals seem to know Spanish as well as their native language, Portuguese. Usually, the best time to visit is between the months of September and October since there it is not during the rainy season and it is not cold. For more information visit their official website.
